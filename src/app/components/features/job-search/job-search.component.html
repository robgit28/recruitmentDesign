Vacancy Search

The most intricate and probably the most important part of your website and we ensure candidates are quickly presented with roles that suit their wants and experience, increasing applications for you.

Fully Furnished Simplicity
Job search functionality can quickly become complicated, providing candidates with too many options. We aim to provide search functionality that presents the most relevant roles with the least amount of decisions and clicks.
Experience
We try our best to make the candidate experience & journey as easy and fluent as possible, allowing candidates to apply for positions without signing up for an account, the ability to send their CV to the recruitment agency, individual recruiters, via your job poster or ATS / CRM.
Flexible
We can give you the functionality to allow candidates to search your vacancies by keyword and / or filter by location, job type, sector, etc. with easy to apply options and calls to action. We can feature the recruiter and their contact details on each vacancy, logos or whatever suits your needs - just let us know.
Convenience
Ease of access to applications, sending them to a general email address or tailoring applications specifically to recruiters.
